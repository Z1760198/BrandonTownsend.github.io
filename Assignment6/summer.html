The Ridges

The Ridges Nature Sanctuary offers wild orchid hikes during the summer months. For more info, visit The Ridges.

Newport State Park

The Newport Wilderness Society sponsors free meadow hikes at 9am every Saturday. Stop by the park office to register.

Summers Flowers

Yellow Lady's Slipper facts: they are 4-24 inches tall; they are perennials; they are a native plant to the area; they grow in wet, shaded deciduous woods, swamps, and bogs; they are of the orchid variety; and they are the official flower of Door County
