Why I play League of Legends

LoL is a team game of 5 on 5 that leaves plenty of room to improve!

I started playing League of Legends as a sophomore in high school, and it was a great way to make friends back then. Ever since though, I've been playing to improve my skill and play better than my friends. I'm a little too competitive, I'm working on it haha. There is a professional e-sports scene for League Of Legends, and the best players make almost as much money as an NFL Pro-Bowler. They are fun to watch, but it's always more fun to play the game yourself with friends!
